BLACK LIVES MATTER

These past couple years have been absolutly tragic. It seemed as if every other week I was watching another black person being murdered by police or another racist person. I once thought that we had become equal from the civil rights act, but I now know that the only thing that happened is that racism has taken another form. Because of the color of my skin I am 3 times the more to die at the hands of a police officer compared to a white man. In addition, 99% of police officers who are involoved in killings do not get charged. Black Lives Matter is a movement for equality. We want to be treated the same as everyone else in this country. We want police officers who abuse their authourity to be held accountable for their actions, we want to change the justice system so it doesn't discriminate against any race, and we want justice for all our brothers and sisters who have died at the hands of police. We have been mistreated for 401 years now. How long must we fight for our lives?
